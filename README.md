## Hey 👋, I'm Mattia Caprio!

I'm a backend developer who started by connecting the dots between systems like databases, pipelines, APIs, and never stopped. Though I'm fully at ease across the stack, from React interfaces down to the data layer.

I specialize in Go services and data engineering: building backends that are lean but resilient, and pipelines that make sense of complex business data.
I like diving deep into structures, business logic and data to understand how things really work — and then building systems that are simple on the surface and solid underneath.

### 🧩 Quick facts

- 🐹 Go developer at heart, full-stack when needed

- 📖 I like understanding how things work under the hood, not just using them

- 🐧 Arch Linux + Hyprland — I spent more time configuring than coding, and I regret nothing

- 🌱 Wrapping my head around design patterns, clean code and the world of cloud & deployment

- ⚡ Fun fact: I debug better at night — the errors are quieter
